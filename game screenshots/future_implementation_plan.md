# Future Implementation Plan - Township Tycoon

This document outlines the remaining phases for the development of Township Tycoon, based on the approved game design roadmap.

## Phase 2: Map Expansion (The 3 New Rooms)
**Goal:** Expand the map to 5 zones, introducing new mechanics and visual variety.

### Zone 3: The Dairy Farm (White/Black Theme)
*   **Unlock Cost:** $15,000
*   **Crop:** Wheat (New crop type).
*   **Entity:** Cows (Animated entities, not static plants).
*   **Mechanic:** **Feed & Produce**. Player/Staff must harvest Wheat -> Feed Cow -> Cow produces Milk.
*   **Machine:** Cheese Press.
*   **Product:** Cheese Wheels ($85).

### Zone 4: The Orchard (Orange/Green Theme)
*   **Unlock Cost:** $50,000
*   **Crop:** Orange Trees.
*   **Mechanic:** **Shake & Collect**. Trees don't disappear; they drop oranges on the ground when interacted with.
*   **Machine:** Juice Blender.
*   **Product:** Orange Juice Cartons ($150).

### Zone 5: The Chocolatery (Purple/Brown Theme)
*   **Unlock Cost:** $150,000
*   **Crop:** Cocoa Pods.
*   **Machine:** Conche Machine (Complex visual).
*   **Product:** Luxury Chocolate Bars ($350).

## Phase 3: The Manager System (RPG Elements)
**Goal:** Add strategy and collection mechanics to staff management.

### Core Mechanics
*   **Manager Cards:** Collectible items with unique names, pixel-art portraits, and passive skills.
*   **Gacha System:** "Briefcases" purchased with **Gems** (earned via Achievements).
*   **Leveling:** Duplicate cards level up the manager, increasing their stats.

### Manager Types
1.  **Production (Green):** Assigned to Machines (e.g., "Luigi the Baker" - +15% Speed).
2.  **Logistics (Blue):** Assigned to Staff (e.g., "Strongman Steve" - +2 Carry Capacity).
3.  **Sales (Gold):** Assigned to Desks (e.g., "The Wolf" - Trucks wait 10s longer).

---

## Phase 4: Audio & Atmosphere
**Goal:** Make the game feel alive and rewarding through sound and visuals.

### Layered Music System
*   Implement a procedural or layered audio system where tracks stack as you unlock zones.
    *   **Room 1:** Bass & Drum (Lo-fi).
    *   **Room 2:** + Synth Melody.
    *   **Room 3:** + High-Hats/Shakers.
    *   **Room 5:** + Harmony/Pad.

### Theme Rewards
*   Unlock global visual themes via Achievements.
    *   **Night Mode:** Unlocked at $50k lifetime earnings.
    *   **Snow Theme:** Unlocked via specific milestones.
    *   **Mars Colony:** Red soil, space helmets.

*   Recurring bill every 10 minutes.
*   Failure to pay pauses production.

## Phase 6: Endgame (Rebirth)
**Goal:** Infinite replayability.

### The Offer
*   Once all 5 rooms are maxed, offer to "Sell the Township".
*   **Reset:** Lose all money, rooms, upgrades.
*   **Gain:** **Prestige Points**.
*   **Effect:** Each point gives +10% permanent profit multiplier.
